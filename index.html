ACM Pune Regional Celebration

11th October 2020

ABOUT

ACM Pune Regional Celebration is an event organized by the Pune Professional ACM Chapter in association with PICT, BVCOE, IOIT, VIIT, and PES Modern College ACM Chapters. The theme of this One Day Event is - Artificial Intelligence. This event is comprised of Expert Session by Distinguished and Eminent Speakers of ACM, followed by a Panel Discussion on Career Options and Planning Your Career after Computer Engineering.
